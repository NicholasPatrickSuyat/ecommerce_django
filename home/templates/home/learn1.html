How It Works

To use AVANT UV coatings, start by preparing the surface, ensuring it is clean and dry, free from any dust, grease, or debris. Evenly apply the AVANT UV coating to the surface, making sure not to over-apply. Allow the coating to settle for about 30 seconds to 1 minute, depending on the specific product you're using. Once the coating has evened out, shine a UV light on it to cure the coating thoroughly, ensuring all areas, including corners and edges, are covered. Check that the entire surface is fully cured to avoid any uncured spots. Finally, depending on your project, sand, polish, or proceed with the next step to achieve the desired finish. Following these steps will ensure a smooth and durable application of AVANT UV coatings.
