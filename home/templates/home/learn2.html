Classical Instrument UV Coatings
The Classical Instrument UV is the hot choice for pianos and guitars. It can be applied for a buffable final finish that looks like polyester. Ideal for guitar necks or restoration.

A UV Curable Classical Instrument Buffable Top Coat that is applied by spray, roll, and brush. For use on wood substrates. Can be applied over pre-finished fully cured substrates. Creates an excellent rubbed finish to replicate classic french polish.
