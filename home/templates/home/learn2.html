Welcome to Our Classical Instruments UV Coatings Information Page

We offer a variety of UV clear coatings and resins designed to provide superior performance and cost-effectiveness. Explore the detailed information about our products, their applications, and how to achieve the best results.

Classical Instrument 830 Buffable Top Coat

UV Curable, applied by spray, roll, and brush. For use on wood substrates. Creates an excellent rubbed finish to replicate classic French polish. Excellent flow and level, excellent adhesion, optically clear, flexible, superior burnishing and buffability, non-yellowing, excellent weathering. Available finishes: Gloss, Satin, Matte. Cost: < $0.40 per SqFt at 1 mil coverage.

Classical Instrument 820 Top Coat

UV Curable, applied by spray, roll, and brush. For use on wood substrates. Excellent flow and level, excellent adhesion, optically clear, flexible, good cut and polish, non-yellowing, excellent weathering. Available finishes: Gloss, Satin, Matte.

Classical Instrument 810 Sealer

UV Curable, applied by spray, roll, and brush. For use on wood substrates. Excellent flow and level, excellent adhesion, optically clear, flexible, easy sand, non-yellowing, excellent weathering, tintable.

Classical Instrument 800 Grain Filler

UV Curable, applied by roller, blade, and squeegee. For use on wood substrates. Excellent leveling, excellent adhesion, optically clear, flexible, non-yellowing, excellent weathering.

How to Use Our Products

  1. Step 1: Prepare the surface by cleaning it thoroughly.
  2. Step 2: Apply the grain filler using a roller or blade.
  3. Step 3: Allow the filler to dry completely.
  4. Step 4: Sand the surface to smooth out any imperfections.
  5. Step 5: Apply the sealer using a spray, roll, or brush.
  6. Step 6: Cure the sealer using UV light.
  7. Step 7: Apply the top coat using a spray, roll, or brush.
  8. Step 8: Cure the top coat using UV light.
  9. Step 9: Buff the surface for a polished finish.
