About Avant

Avant is at the forefront of the coatings industry, introducing UV-curable technology that sets new standards in cleanliness, strength, and speed. Our products are designed to meet the demanding needs of various industries, offering unparalleled benefits for both commercial and personal applications.

Our advanced coatings are VOC and HAP-free, ensuring a safe environment for users and the surrounding ecosystem. With Avant, experience coatings that not only perform exceptionally but also contribute positively to environmental sustainability.

Key Features

Five Minute Cure Time

Avant’s patented coating systems provide the fastest finish times available! Eliminate expensive curing steps and reduce throughput bottlenecks with our efficient low energy LED UVA-lighting system. Fully cures within minutes under UVA-lighting or direct sunlight.

VOC/HAP Free

Our coatings have a low carbon footprint, offering cost savings and environmental benefits. No hazardous disposal is required, and equipment cleans up easily with isopropyl alcohol and soap and water.

Non-Shrinking 100% Solids Coating

Avant’s formulations provide superior surface hardness, flexibility, and resistance to scratches, chemicals, and weathering. Our coatings offer excellent adhesion and self-healing properties, ensuring a long-lasting, clear finish that resists yellowing.
