Avant UV Coatings Touch-Up Kit

The Avant UV Coatings Touch-Up Kit is designed for professionals and hobbyists needing to repair and maintain UV coatings with precision and ease. This all-in-one kit includes everything required to perform high-quality touch-ups on UV-coated surfaces.

The Insides

The Avant UV Coatings Touch-Up Kit includes essential tools for repairing and touching up UV coatings. This comprehensive kit features a small jar of UV paste, a syringe of UV curable touch-up paste, a handheld curing light, sanding block and paper, essential tools, and cleaning alcohol, all neatly packaged in a lightweight, portable case.

Includes

UV Paste Jar and Syringe: A small jar and syringe of UV curable touch-up paste for precise repairs.
Handheld Curing Light: A compact UV curing light to ensure the paste cures quickly and effectively.
Sanding Block and Paper: For smoothing surfaces before and after application of the UV paste.
Essential Tools: Includes razors, Dixie cups, and other tools needed for application and mixing.
IPA Alcohol: Isopropyl alcohol for cleaning resin and preparing surfaces.
Lightweight Carry Case: A portable, easy-to-carry case to keep all components organized and ready for use.

Uses

This kit is designed for convenience and efficiency, providing all the tools needed to make professional-grade repairs and touch-ups on a variety of UV coated surfaces. Whether you’re working on furniture, automotive parts, or fine crafts, the Avant UV Coatings Touch-Up Kit ensures you have the right tools for a flawless finish.

  • Comprehensive Tool Set: The kit includes high-quality tools designed for precision and durability, making it perfect for repairing and enhancing furniture, automotive parts, and delicate crafts.
  • Versatile Applications: Whether you’re working on wooden furniture, car surfaces, or intricate crafts, our kit provides the versatility needed to achieve a seamless and flawless finish.
  • User-Friendly: Each tool is designed with ease of use in mind, allowing both professionals and DIY enthusiasts to achieve expert-level results without the need for extensive training.
  • Durability and Quality: The Avant UV Coatings Touch-Up Kit is built to last, with materials that withstand frequent use and deliver consistent, high-quality results.

Kit Materials

The Avant UV Coatings Touch-Up Kit is available in a basic size, perfect for general repairs and touch-ups. For those with specific needs, custom kit options are available—please contact Avant UV Coatings to tailor a kit to your exact requirements.

Starter Kit

The Starter Kit is ideal for small-scale projects and individual use. It contains all the essential tools needed for minor touch-ups and repairs on UV-coated surfaces.

Intermediate Kit

The Intermediate Kit is designed for medium-sized projects and frequent use. It provides a more comprehensive set of tools for a variety of repair and maintenance tasks on UV-coated surfaces.

Pro Kit

The Pro Kit is perfect for large-scale projects and professional use. It offers an extensive set of high-quality tools and materials to handle complex and large touch-up and repair tasks on UV-coated surfaces.
